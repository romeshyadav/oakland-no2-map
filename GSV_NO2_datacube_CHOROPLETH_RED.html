Sources & Notes
  • WHO Global Air Quality Guidelines (2021): NO₂ 24‑hour AQG = 25 µg/m³; annual AQG = 10 µg/m³. The 2005 1‑hour guideline of 200 µg/m³ was not re‑evaluated in 2021.
  • US EPA NAAQS (primary): NO₂ 1‑hour = 100 ppb (98th percentile of daily max 1‑hour, 3‑year average); annual = 53 ppb.
  • Unit conversion: ppb ≈ 24.45 × (µg/m³) ÷ MW at 25 °C & 1 atm (NO₂ MW≈46) ⇒ 200 µg/m³ ≈ 106 ppb.
